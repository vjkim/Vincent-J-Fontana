A website on Child Abuse Awareness.

First, we need to understand what Child Abuse is.

    Child Abuse is:

  • when a parent or caregiver, whether through action or failing to act, causes injury, death, emotional harm or risk of serious harm to a child.

    • This can occur in many forms. This includes

  • Neglect
  • Physical Abuse
  • Sexual Abuse
  • Exploitation
  • Emotional Abuse
